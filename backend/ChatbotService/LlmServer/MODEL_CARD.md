## Intended Use

**Primary use:** AI sales assistant chatbot for OKLA, a vehicle marketplace platform in the Dominican Republic.

**Users:** End customers browsing vehicles on okla.com.do

**Out of scope:**

- Legal, financial, or insurance advice
- Medical or personal advice
- Use outside of the OKLA vehicle marketplace context
- Languages other than Spanish (will respond in Spanish)

## Limitations

1. **Domain-specific:** Only trained for vehicle sales conversations
2. **Inventory-dependent:** Responses about vehicles are grounded on dealer inventory injected via RAG
3. **CPU inference:** 2-5 minutes per response on CPU-only servers
4. **Context window:** 4,096 tokens — long conversations may lose early context
5. **Single language:** Optimized for Dominican Spanish; may not handle other Spanish dialects well
6. **No real-time data:** Cannot access external APIs or live pricing

## Safety & Ethical Considerations

- **PII Detection:** Built-in PII sanitization (cédula, credit cards, emails)
- **Prompt Injection:** 28 pattern-based detection rules
- **Legal Compliance:** Refuses legal/financial advice, redirects to professionals
- **Anti-Hallucination:** Responses grounded on real inventory data
- **Interaction Limits:** 10/session, 50/user/day, 100K/global/month

## How to Update This Card

When training a new model version:

1. Update `model-registry.json` with new version entry
2. Fill in evaluation results after running `evaluate_before_deploy.py`
3. Update the version number at the top of this card
4. Record the dataset SHA256 hash for traceability
